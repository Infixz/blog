vsftp 启用被动模式（passive mode）解决客户端"no route to host"错误



vsftpd.conf:

pasv_min_port=12000
pasv_max_port=12199
pasv_enable=YES


iptables:

iptables -I INPUT -p tcp --dport 12000:12199 -j ACCEPT
service iptables save
